Music

I love music, but I love recommending music even more. Here are some of my music recommendations:

  • Frank Ocean

    Frank Ocean is known for his alternative R&B and rap style of music. My favorite songs from him include:

    1. Bad Religion
    2. Nights
    3. Seigfried

  • Tyler the Creator

    Tyler the Creator is known for his alternative hip-hop style of music. My favorite songs from him include:

    1. Sorry Not Sorry
    2. St. Chroma
    3. Answer

  • Ethel Cain

    Ethel Cain is known for her southern goth, indie rock, and contemporary folk style of music. My favorite songs from her include:

    1. Thoroughfare
    2. A House In Nebraska
    3. Punish

  • Blood Orange

    Blood Orange is known for his alternative R&B and experimental jazz style of music. My favorite songs from him include:

    1. Chewing Gum
    2. Champagne Coast
    3. Charcoal Baby

  • Yebba

    Yebba is known for her R&B and neo-jazz style of music. My favorite songs from her include:

    1. Louie Bag
    2. Waterfall
    3. One More Smile
